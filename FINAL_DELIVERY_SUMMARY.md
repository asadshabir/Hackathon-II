## 🎨 **DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS APPLIED**

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: Indigo-600 (#4F46E5)
- **Accent**: Teal-500 (#14B8A6)
- **Backgrounds**: White to Slate-100 gradients
- **Dark Mode**: Slate-800 to Slate-900 gradients

### **Visual Effects**
- **Shadows**: Custom neumorphic shadows (8px 8px 16px + -8px -8px 16px)
- **Transitions**: ≤200ms with `cubic-bezier(0.4,0,0.2,1)` easing
- **Scale Effects**: Limited to 1.02-1.04 range
- **Translate Effects**: Limited to -2px to -4px range

### **Mobile Optimization**
- 3D effects disabled on screens <768px
- Touch-friendly targets (44px minimum)
- Optimized transitions for mobile performance
- Responsive layouts preserved
